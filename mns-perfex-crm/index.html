Create your desktop application from your Perfex CRM
Instructions
  • Perfex CRM URL: Full URL of your Perfex CRM.
  • Application Name: Desired name for your application.
  • Width: Desired width of the application window.
  • Height: Desired height of the application window.
  • App Icon: Upload the desired icon for your application. Accepts formats .png, .ico, and .icns.
  • Title Bar Style: Style of the application's title bar.
  • Platform: Platform on which the application should be generated.
